TREATMENT

Starting Your Treatment Cycle

Day 1 Protocol

When your period starts:

1. Call +1 (416) 586-4748 or email: msfmessage.msh@sinaihealth.ca on your first full flow day.

2. Contact the nurses (not administrative staff).

3. Receive your personalized cycle instructions.

Patient Portal

Personalized Patient Portal:

1. Your instructions will be delivered via a secure portal.

2. Text notifications: "You have a message in the Mount Sinai Fertility patient portal".

3. Always log in to review complete instructions.

Portal access: https://fertilitymountsinai.ca/PatientPortal

POLICIES

Appointment Policies

Next Appointment

Upon receipt of the personalized investigations plan from Rachel make sure to:

  • Confirm your next appointment date (or request to reschedule) within 48 hours.
  • Complete all investigations before the next visit.
Translation

Translation services are available for all languages.


Please inform Rachel if you require a translator for your next appointment.

Cancellation Policy

48-hour minimum notice required!

Cancellation fees (if less than 48 hours notice):

  • Review appointments: $80
  • New patient appointments: $140
  • Payment is required before rescheduling.

We understand life happens -
Please contact us as soon as you know you need to reschedule!
